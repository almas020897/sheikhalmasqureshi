GWBL Quote/Exercise Implementation

wmA (Mainframe)
March 2024 to December 2024

Summary~

The GWBL Max Maturity process to be established for Contracts, so that GWBL Max Maturity elections VALO/VALP can be processed. This provides user options of GWBL max maturity quote and GWBL max maturity exercise. GWBL max maturity quote (VALO)– Valuation quote enabled up to a year prior to and 30 days beyond max maturity for Accumulator Series products. GWBL max maturity exercise (VALP) – When client with GWBL Accum contract who has reached max maturity age are enabled to make max-maturity election, so that my supplemental contract can be setup based on the annuitization option that best suits user needs. Transaction used for annuitization will be VALP. Screen is available for Accum GWBL contracts that have been sent all required maturity letters and only until a maturity option is executed. Available Options are: • GWBL for the GWBL Benefit Annuitization Option • LIFE for the Life Annuity with Zero Period Certain Option
